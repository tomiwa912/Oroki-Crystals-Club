Our Distinguished Members

Meet the dedicated professionals and leaders of Oroki Crystals Club, united in service and community.

Executive Leadership

Our dedicated executive team guiding the club's mission and vision

Alhaji (Engr.) Muideen Bello

President

From Osogbo, he is an accomplished engineer and entrepreneur with extensive experience at major telecommunications firms including MTN Nigeria. He brings strong leadership skills and strategic vision to his role as President of Oroki Crystals Club. His interests in politics, history, and faith inform his commitment to community development and cultural preservation.

Comrade Adebowale Adekola

Vice President

From Osogbo, he serves as an Auditor at the Office of The Auditor General for Local Governments in Osun State. As pioneer Vice President and co-founder of Oroki Crystals Club, he provides strategic oversight and financial accountability. His professional expertise in auditing ensures transparent governance and fiscal responsibility within the organization.

Pharm. Ropo Ogunwale

Publicity Secretary

From Osogbo, he is a community pharmacist and healthcare entrepreneur with multiple pharmaceutical businesses. He serves as the club's Publicity Secretary, leveraging his expertise in health and community development. His strategic approach enhances the organization's public engagement and outreach initiatives.

Engr. Salami Abiodun Olalekan

Treasurer

From Ibadan, he is a construction engineer and project manager at Lanfar Nigeria Limited. He brings his engineering expertise and project management skills to his role as the club's Treasurer. His professional background ensures meticulous financial oversight and responsible resource management for the organization.

Crystal Taiwo Oyebamiji

Assistant General Secretary

From Otan Ayegbaju, he has professional experience working with the Nigerian Postal Service (NIPOST). His interests include politics, sports, and herbal studies, and he possesses skills in entrepreneurship and trading.

Crystal Olalekan Komolafe

Social Secretary

From Ijebu Jesa, Oriade LG, he is the CEO of Divine Favour Farms in Osogbo and also leads Komo Builders & Properties. His interests include music, meeting people, community service, and traveling.
